🏮 How It Works

Speed networking — boba style!

1

Panelists Take Their Corners

Each featured panelist sets up in a different corner of the room, ready to share their experience and insights.

2

Break Into Groups

Attendees are split into small breakout groups and assigned to a panelist to begin the conversation.

3

Rotate Every 10–15 Min

When time is up, groups rotate to the next panelist's corner — making sure everyone gets a chance to connect!

4

Sip & Enjoy 🧋

Grab your boba and make the most of every conversation. New connections await!
